Overview

GitHub Stats Generator respects intellectual property rights and expects its users to do the same. This policy outlines our procedures for responding to notices of alleged copyright infringement in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act ("DMCA").

DMCA Notice Requirements

To file a copyright infringement notification, please send a written communication that includes:

1

A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized agent

2

Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed

3

Identification of the infringing material and its location

4

Your contact information (name, address, telephone number, email)

5

A statement of your good faith belief that the use is not authorized

6

A statement of accuracy and authorization under penalty of perjury

Counter Notice

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter notice containing:

Your physical or electronic signature

Identification of the removed material

A statement under penalty of perjury of your good faith belief

Your contact information and consent to legal jurisdiction
